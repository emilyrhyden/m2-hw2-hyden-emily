Hobbies

I don't have much free time - I work full time at a company I have to commute to every day. However, when I do have free time, writing, climbing, and playing music are three activities that bring me comfort and joy.

Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is playing flute. I have played since I was 12 years old. I am currently a member of the San Francisco Civic Symphony. My most recent concert was Sunday, August 25th. I performed my favorite piece: Dvorak's New World Symphony. Playing music makes me feel alive and grounded in the present moment.

Interests

I am interested in economics, politics, history, and the intersections of the three. I enjoy writing analytical essays about such topics and follow along closely with both domestic and international politics. I studied International Studies & Economics in undergrad and minored in Chinese. My thesis explored Chinese foreign aid mechanisms and their alignment with Confucian principles, examining how these aid practices reflect and promote traditional values.

Career Goals

Over the span of my career, I would love to oversee communications strategy for a major campaign or political advocacy group. I would also like to open my own communications consulting firm one day and become a trusted sage in my field. The end goal of course is to retire young knowing I left a mark on the world!
